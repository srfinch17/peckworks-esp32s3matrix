Fill: one LED per interval, bottom→top, yellow→red by default.
Snow: flakes fall and accumulate randomly per row, blue→white by default.
Digits: large number countdown with a second-progress bar, green→red.
All timers flash for 10 seconds when they expire, then stay lit.
